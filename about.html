Our Story

Turning a genuine love for baking into a community centerpiece.

Humble Beginnings

T's Cakes and Pastries began in 2024 when our founder turned a passionate hobby into a small business. It started simply with homemade cakes, signature muffins, and hot buttermilk scones.

Initially baked exclusively for friends, family, and local events, word quickly spread. Today, we are proud to be known throughout the community for our deeply uncompromised quality, locally sourced ingredients, and treats made with genuine love.

Our Vision

To be the absolute premiere bakery that our community thinks of first when they want fresh, delicious, completely unforgettable treats.

Our Mission

We are entirely committed to bringing people together through high-quality baked goods. We focus on perfect ingredients, brilliant service, and making every single customer feel special.
