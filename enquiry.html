How Our Ordering Process Works

To ensure maximum freshness and quality, please keep the following timeframes in mind when making an enquiry:

  • General Pastries and Breads: Please enquire at least 24 hours in advance for bulk orders.
  • Custom Celebration Cakes: We require a minimum of 4 to 7 days notice depending on the complexity of the custom design.
  • Funerals and Large Events: Please contact us as soon as possible so we can immediately prioritize your community catering needs.

Once you submit the enquiry form below, our customer service team will review your order and contact you directly within a few hours to confirm all details, dates, and pricing.
