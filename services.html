Important Ordering & Allergy Information

Allergy Declarations

All of our exquisite products are baked in a shared commercial bakery. While our staff takes every strict precaution to prevent cross-contamination, please be acutely aware that our products may contain or come into contact with common allergens including wheat, peanuts, tree nuts, milk, eggs, soybeans, and sesame. Please explicitly state any severe allergies when placing a custom order so we can isolate your bake.

Preparation Lead Times

We deeply believe in baking everything absolutely fresh, directly from scratch. Therefore, we firmly require a minimum of 48 hours notice for all standard signature cake and pastry orders. Massive corporate orders, tiered wedding cakes, and catering spreads require a strict minimum of three weeks notice to secure your date and ensure we source the absolutely finest ingredients from our global suppliers.

Deposit & Cancellation Policy

For custom catering orders exceeding R1,000, a 50% non-refundable deposit is explicitly required at the time of booking to safely secure your production slot. The remaining balance is due exactly 24 hours before your scheduled pickup or vehicle delivery time. If you experience an emergency and need to cancel, please notify our logistics team at least one week in advance.
